Mission Structure, For Builders
Mission elements are created dynamically on load by markers placed during the building phase, below are the various markers to dictate how and where things happen.
- Multiple drop off sites to the AO may be defined with predefined requirements:
	- "Pickup" Helicopter Drop off site (Only an option when helicopter is owned by group)
	- "hd_pickup" Vehicle Drop off site (Only an option when a vehicle is owned)
	- "Start" Walking Drop off site (Always an option, provide at least 3)
- Mission objectives can be:
	- "Destroy" Destroy Object 
	- "Flag" Capture Area
